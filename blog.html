About the Artist

Rony Bolivar

Founder of the Art movement ‘Fluidalism’ I am a digital artist passionate about vintage aesthetics, baroque elegance, drama, and epic abstract themes. My work draws from historical art masters like Kandinsky among others and personal inspirations from fantasy literature and history. Each piece is created with attention to detail, blending traditional techniques with modern digital tools. Available as high-resolution digital downloads, prints, and custom commissions. Born and Raised in Caracas, Venezuela but Based in New York, since 2012 I create art that tells stories of resilience, beauty, and mystery.
